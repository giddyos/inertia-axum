# Askama root support final audit

This record audits the implementation against
`specs/20260711-174925-askama-root-template-support/00-overview.md` and Phases
01 through 10. The audit completed on 2026-07-11.

## Result

All source requirements and acceptance criteria are implemented or verified.
Two audit-time gaps were fixed: security guidance was placed directly beside
the remaining rustdoc/SSR `safe` examples, and the test-only Askama fallible
filter received a narrowly scoped allowance for lint attributes generated by
`askama::filter_fn`. No requirement remains unverifiable.
